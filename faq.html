PRODUCT QUESTIONS

What are the ingredients in your beverages?

Our beverages contain carefully selected natural ingredients. Each flavor has its unique blend:

Zeera: Carbonated water, sugar, cumin extract, citric acid, natural flavors, and spices.

Nimbu Masala: Carbonated water, sugar, lemon juice, salt, mint, ginger, and traditional masala spices.

Lychee: Carbonated water, sugar, lychee essence, natural flavors, and citric acid.

Blueberry: Carbonated water, sugar, blueberry essence, natural flavors, and citric acid.

Mojito: Carbonated water, sugar, lime extract, mint essence, and natural flavors.

All our products are GST and FSSAI registered.

Are your products suitable for diabetics?

Our beverages contain sugar and are not specifically formulated for diabetics. We recommend consulting with a healthcare professional before consuming our products if you have diabetes. We are exploring options for sugar-free variants in the future.

How should I store The Real Ratlami beverages?

For best quality and taste:

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Refrigerate after opening for maximum freshness
  • Consume within 3 days of opening
  • Best before date is printed on the bottle cap
  • Do not freeze as it may affect the carbonation
Do your products contain preservatives?

We use minimal preservatives as required for safety and shelf stability. Our products contain sodium benzoate and potassium sorbate in quantities well within FSSAI guidelines. These preservatives are commonly used in the beverage industry and are safe for consumption.
