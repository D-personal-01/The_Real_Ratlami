Our Beginnings

The Real Ratlami was born from a simple idea: to bring the authentic taste of Ratlam to every home across India. Founded in the historic city of Ratlam, Madhya Pradesh, our journey began with a passion for preserving traditional Indian flavors in a modern, accessible format.

Inspired by the bustling markets and vibrant street food culture of Ratlam, we set out to create beverages that capture the essence of desi taste - bold, refreshing, and unmistakably Indian.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great taste comes from great ingredients. That's why we source only the finest, real ingredients for our beverages. From premium zeera seeds to fresh fruit essences, every component is carefully selected to ensure the authentic taste that defines The Real Ratlami.

Our manufacturing process combines traditional techniques with modern quality standards, ensuring that every bottle delivers the same consistent, delicious experience that has made us a household name.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our commitment to authentic taste and quality remains unwavering. We're constantly innovating to bring new flavors while staying true to our roots, and expanding our reach to bring the taste of Ratlam to more households across India.
